Frequently Asked Questions

- 1. Do you only sell GreenB bikes?

Our business does focus mainly on GreenB bikes, but we also sell different kinds of bikes such as mountain bikes.

- 2. I want to customise my bike, what can I do to make sure I have the correct parts?

You can talk to a customer representative to help you pick out the parts either online or at our local store. This means we can get to know the type of bike you want, and we can then help you pick out the right parts.

- 3. Is it possible for me to pick up after the purchase?

Absolutely. You may pick up your bike parts at a local GreenB store or you may wait for it to ship to your doorsteps!

- 4. What kind of bike should I buy?

We know picking a type of bike can be confusing at first, but our GreenB staff are able to help you find the correct type of bike. You may talk to an online customer representative for assistance or you may visit our local store.
