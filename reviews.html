GT Garothic 3028

The GT Garothic 3028 is a dual suspension fun machine, 130/120mm of travel Front/Rear let’s you power fearlessly through harsh sections with absolute confidence.

The 220mm Tiger CTD rear shock paired with the Tiger 34 Float, 130mm fork provide plush, fully tuneable travel making the GT Garothic 3028 hugely capable and massive fun on descents.

A great combination of well chosen components including GT Garothic hydraulic disc brakes, Zero seatpost, bar and stem and Sram10 speed groupset make the Coppermine a freerider’s dream bike. a total weapon on the descents and still able to make the climbs.
